Click and hold to go slowmmo. Spacebar to reset. The bateria will be attracted to the mouse. Keyboard controls are in [brackets] next to what they control. The attraction variable is completely independant of speed. The bacteria's movement will be a weighted avergae of where the bacteria "wants to go" (randomly) and where it has to go to get to your mouse. 1 means equal weighting, 0 means the bacteria don't even care about the mouse.
